About Our Department

The Department of Basic Science & Humanities was established in 1995 as a cornerstone of our institution. We provide essential foundational knowledge to all first-year engineering students across disciplines.

Our department houses state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty members, and a curriculum designed to prepare students for their specialized engineering studies.

The department is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and fostering critical thinking skills that are vital for future engineers.

We focus on developing not just technical knowledge but also communication skills, ethical understanding, and environmental awareness that are crucial for well-rounded professionals.
